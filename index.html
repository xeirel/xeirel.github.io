Who is Xeirel

Xeirel is an independent game developer primarily working with Unity and C#. With a strong focus on gameplay systems, multiplayer mechanics, and performance-oriented development, Xeirel has been actively creating and publishing games and interactive experiences.

What is Xeirel Software?

Xeirel Software is a small independent game development studio consisting of two developers. The studio is behind the games No Strangers, Digital Market Simulator, and SEVENS, all developed using Unity.

What we also do?

In addition to game development, Xeirel Software also develops internal tools and utilities, including X Utils, created to support and streamline development workflows in Unity.
